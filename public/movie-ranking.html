Movie Recommendations

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Silence of the Lambs

The FBI is on the trail of a serial killer with a dark secret. What happens when their only hope of solving the case involves the twisted mind of Dr. Hannibal Lector?

Gladiator

From General to slave and slave to Gladiator - Can one man really rise to become the Champion and Protector of Rome?

Wall-E

Join one robot's journey to bring humans back to Earth, and maybe teach them a thing or two about sustainability in the process.
